Getting started

Groups within a team

Not every user needs to see every post. By creating groups within the team, the coach can target specific types of users when posting in the news feed. Perhaps you want to give a message to the other coaches? Or maybe give some feedback to your defenders, commenting their performance last match?

Events

Creating events is a brilliant way of getting attendance statistics for your team. It can be a one-time event or sessions that are being automatically created every week, just as you want it. Let the athletes report if they are coming to the session, or fill it in by yourself. Either way it will show up in the statistics page.
